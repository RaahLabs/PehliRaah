Game 2 of 10
🤝 Bharosa
bharosa — trust

You're paired with a stranger — an AI — and neither of you can talk. Each round, you both make a move at the same time: work together, or look out for yourself. If you both cooperate, you both win something. But if you cooperate and they don't, you get nothing while they pocket more. The AI watches every move you make and adjusts its strategy round by round. Ten rounds. No chat. Just choices.

A bigger disc can never sit on a smaller one. Sounds manageable until you're five moves deep and realize every path forward requires undoing what you just did. You have three minutes. An undo is allowed, but it costs you a move.
